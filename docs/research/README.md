# Research & Incident Write-ups

Dated investigation notes, incident analyses, and external-feature reviews, named `YYYY-MM-DD-<slug>.md` (date the investigation happened).

These are point-in-time records: they capture what was known/true at the time (hardware, versions, upstream bugs) and are not maintained afterward. Cross-link them from the relevant living doc (e.g. `docs/TROUBLESHOOTING.md` Known Issues) instead of updating them.
