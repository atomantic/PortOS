## Task

Rewrite the draft to satisfy the brief. This is a **revision, not a rewrite from
scratch**: preserve the plot, characters, structure, and the passages the brief
protects. Tighten and sharpen — cut over-explanation and redundancy, let action
and dialogue carry meaning, and honor the target length. Do NOT add new plot
events, characters, or scenes the original didn't have. Do NOT pad back the fat
the brief asked you to trim.

Match the original's tense, point of view, and voice precisely.

## Output contract

Return ONLY the revised prose. No preamble, no commentary, no markdown fence, no
notes about what you changed — just the finished revised draft text.
